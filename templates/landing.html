How it works inside a practice

When a patient cancels, SwiftSlot broadcasts the open slot to your consented short‑notice list. The first confirmed response locks the appointment automatically.

1 — Cancellation occurs Reception marks the slot as available.
2 — Immediate broadcast SwiftSlot notifies your waitlist instantly.
3 — Slot secured The first confirmed patient locks the appointment.
